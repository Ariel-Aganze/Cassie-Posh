Cassie Posh Events is a full-service wedding planning and event design company based in Philadelphia, proudly serving clients throughout the Tri-State area. At Cassie Posh Events, sophisticated design meets seamless celebration. We work with couples who desire more than just a wedding — they seek an unforgettable experience that feels deeply personal, beautifully curated, and effortlessly elevated.

Every celebration begins with your story, and we shape it with meticulous detail, imaginative artistry, and flawless execution.
